This project focuses on crime data visualization of the great Seattle area from 2011-2015. We can see how crime amount and proportion changed across years, and what are the relationships between active business, population, and crime amount. Before visualization, some data cleaning works are done to achieve these charts.

  • Data Source
    • - Crime Data: Seattle Open Data. This dataset describe counties' crime type and amount happened in a specific month.
    - Active Business License: Seattle Open Data. Active Business License is used to measure the economic development level in one area in this project. It has the granularity on year and interpolation techologies are used to match the crime data.
  • Chart Description
    • - Line and Area Chart: It describes how different types of crime amount changed across years.
    - Column and Bar Chart: It describes how different types of crime proportions changed across years.
    - Scatter and Bubble Chart: It describes how number of active business license, population and crime amount relate to each other, and each bubble is labeled with year.
